Mario's Italian Ice Review
Mauricio Gonzalez
Columbia University in The City of New York Rising Senior

If you ever find yourself in Chicago and are looking for a delicious refreshing treat, you can not pass up on Mario's Italian Ice!


It's a small little stand located in Little Italy - 1068 W Taylor St, Chicago, IL 60607.

What makes Mario's Italian Ice so special is that they use natural ingredients rather than artifical sweetners!
That is something that I personally look out for as I am trying to fix my diet and eat healthier than I have in the past.


Here my friend and I got a blue raspberry and raspberry ice tea.
It was 80 degrees outside and this was the perfect treat to cool off and have a unique desert that is healthy and tasty!

I can not stress enough how good this place is! If you are ever in town or visiting, you must put this on top of your to do list!
